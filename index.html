My Projects

Knex Knews (back-end)

An API featuring data on topics, articles, users and comments in the style of Reddit. It consists of a relational database and a server, to which the user can make a variety of requests. A hosted version can be found via this link.


The project utilizes the following: JavaScript, Express, PostgreSQL, Knex, Jest & Supertest.

Knex Knews (front-end)

A React web app allowing users to interact with the news by reading & creating articles, posting comments, voting on content and more! This project was bootstrapped with Create React App. The github repository can be found via this link, while the hosted version can be found here.

This project utilizes the following: JavaScript, React, CSS & Axios.

About Me

My story so far...

Hello world, I’m Alex - a coder and trainee software developer from Leeds. I am currently a Northcoders student enrolled on the remote bootcamp, an experience that has sparked a deep love of programming and a tolerant indifference to Zoom.

I was previously a sports performance analyst - a position that allowed me to travel to different countries and work with lots of interesting people. This is also where I discovered a passion for problem solving and creative thinking! As someone who has always had an enthusiasm for technology and an obsession over detail, learning to code felt like the obvious next step to take as I sought a new challenge in my working life. Having spoken to a couple of previous Northcoders graduates, the glow on their (digital) faces when they spoke about their bootcamp experience and subsequent roles was all the convincing I needed to take the leap!

I love collaborating with others on projects, something that was nurtured as I grew up playing in a variety of different musical outfits - I’ve played percussion in jazz groups and guitar in noisy metal bands! My other big source of joy in life is cricket; while I am a pretty ordinary batsman even on my better days, the sport has instilled in me the importance of a positive mindset, fairness, honesty and respect for others. These are all values I bring to my professional life as well, making me a valuable team member and amiable colleague.

Feel free to get in touch, I’m very easy-going (as long as I’ve had a cup of coffee!). You can click the contact icons below.
